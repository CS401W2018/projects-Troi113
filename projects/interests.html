Hoops!

Profile Interest Hero

I was never a sporty individual when I was young. Always been the one with butter-fingers. That's why it's so unbelievable to me how I became such a big basketball fan in my early to mid 20's. I never missed a week not playing basketball since picking it up straight after my mission.

It is a game that involves all aspects of human fitness, including dexterity, agility, strength, and speed. Alongside that, it is also a mental game, requiring teamwork, creativity, strategy and emotional strength. The purpose of this paper is to talk about how a humble recreational game has evolved into a beautiful physical activity that is enjoyed all around the world.

It has also produced star athletes such as Michael Jordan, Kobe Bryant, Lebron James, Larry Bird, Wilt Chamberlain, Kareem Abdul-Jabbar, and the like. I am more interested in watching high school players though. I love the thrilling energy that these young bloods bring to the court.
