Kyrie Irving

Profile Interest Hero

Often times, the way a man moves describe the way he thinks. A man that thinks with purpose moves with purpose. A purposeful man thinks thoughtfully, but when it's time to move he is decisive and acts with courage and confidence.

When I was a kid I did not know how to play basketball. I was the kid on the side playing video games and avoiding interactions. But I've always been a basketball fan. And now that I started playing a sport and observing professional players, the more I became enchanted to the art of movement.

Kyrie Irving is an american basketball player that moves with brilliance. He is a point guard - or the floor general - of his team. He leads the team by being the primary ball handler and creates plays so that the team can win against opponents. What makes his game mind blowing are these following elements:

Ball Handling
Kyrie has the ball on a string. His elite ball-handling skills allows him to move freely around the court, in every direction.
Court Vision
Kyrie sees all. His court vision and focus allows him to see tactics that he could use to bring the ball to the opponent's rim.
Finishing at the Rim
Kyrie is a slasher that tears through defenses. The combination of his fleet footwork, ball handling and relentless arm movements clears through walls of defenders and brings him close to the rim to score
Deadly in Iso
Kyrie's highschool, college, and NBA experience has molded him into a formidable scorer. Bring him in an island against aman and he will survive, every, single, time.
