One Million Royale

1 Million royale by Paco Rabanne is an Oriental Woody Fragrance. It has been released and launch in 2023. Its main perfumers were Christophe Raynaud and Quentin Bisch

"Powered by your inner confidence, enter the 1 Million Royal kingdom with the ultimate fragrance of defiant self-expression, made for you by Paco Rabanne.

Unconstrained by convention, find the freedom to live by your own rules with 1 Million Royal: a flamboyant fresh woody blend that collides with a distinctive sensuality to unleash what makes you uniquely you.

Note Breakdown

Notes

Middle Notes
Lavander Sage Violet Leaf
Base Notes
Benzoin Cedar Patchouli

Rating

I would give an overall rating of 4.8/5

Best Season

Best season for this fragrance would be Fall. It has an oriental, woody, and sweet notes that might be polarizing for the summer. This is perfect for cold weather, especially for date nights.

Gender

This leans towards extremely masculine

Sillage

Based on experience, I need to spray twice on a spot in order for the sillage to cover a good area. I would put the Sillage rating on moderate

Performance

The scent is long-lasting. I spray on it one time for a date and went to bed with the scent. I woke up and the scent still lingers.
