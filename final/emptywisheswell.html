Empty Wishes Well

Toskovat is a young, upcoming perfume manufacturer. It's main nose is David-Lev Jipa-Slivinschi, who started his perfumer debut in 2022. One of his first fragrances was Empty Wishes Well. This fragrance is publicly acclaimed as a niche and unique scent.

This purfume follows a storytelling of a rusted coin below a dried up wishing well. Jipa-Slivinschi's vision was to recreate a scenery that embodies hope that someday, the empty well will be doused in water that life will continue on.

Rating

I would give an overall rating of 3.5/5

Here are some pointers what explains my rating:

Pros

  • Unique scent, probably nothing you smelled before
  • Leans slightly masculine
  • Smell of moss and damp rain that might be satisfying to some
  • Sage and vetiver adds a very green and fresh feeling
  • Deep, non-linear scent that changes as time goes

Cons

  • Not a compliment getter. wore this a couple of times and the scent never got noticed
  • Might smell polarizing and old-fashioned for some because of the rusty coin note.
  • Super overpriced. One full bottle is $260.
  • Overpowering petrichor that might cause dizzyness or headache to some

In my opinion, the pros does not compensate the risk. It is a very difficult fragrance to go about. But in conclusion, this fragrance is a unique green fragrances that is still very wearable despite its ability to be overpowering.

Best Season

This is a fall and spring fragrance. It invokes a earthy, green, and mossy scent that might not match summer or winter seasons very well.

Gender

This leans towards slightly masculine

Sillage

Based on experience, I need to spray twice on a spot in order for the sillage to cover a good area. I would put the Sillage rating on moderate

Performance

The scent is long-lasting on my skin(overnight), but might also be extremely long-lasting depending on skin types.What I mean by that is that it could last for a few days. I sprayed it on a corner of a room one time and the scen t lingered for three days.
